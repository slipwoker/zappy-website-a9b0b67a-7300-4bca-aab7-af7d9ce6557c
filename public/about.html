We turn workplace noise into navigable signals.

In a world flooded with opinions, ratings, and reviews, finding clarity about where to work—or how to improve your workplace—can feel like searching for a signal in static. At Goblin Shark, we believe that every employee's voice matters, and every company deserves fair, transparent feedback that drives real change.

Our mission is to cut through the noise and surface the insights that matter most. By combining advanced methodology with bias-aware design, we transform raw workplace feedback into actionable intelligence that empowers job seekers and employers alike. We're not just another review platform—we're a compass for navigating the modern workplace landscape with confidence and clarity.

Readable by default

We present data in clear, digestible formats that anyone can understand—no PhD required. Complex salary comparison tools and workplace metrics are distilled into intuitive visualizations that tell the story at a glance.

Bias-aware by design

We actively work to identify and mitigate bias in our data collection and presentation. Our algorithms are built with fairness at their core, ensuring that every voice—regardless of role, tenure, or background—is weighted appropriately.

Trust through methodology

Transparency isn't optional—it's foundational. We openly share how we collect, verify, and analyze workplace data. Our methodology is rigorous, peer-reviewed, and continuously refined to maintain the highest standards of accuracy and reliability.

Built like a map: structured, consistent, and honest.

At Goblin Shark, our values guide every decision we make—from how we collect feedback to how we present it. We believe transparency and clarity are essential for meaningful workplace insights.

Evidence over hype

We prioritize verified data and authentic employee experiences over marketing spin. Every review is validated, every rating backed by real workplace insights.

Respect for privacy

Your feedback remains confidential. We protect contributor anonymity while ensuring employers can learn from constructive criticism without compromising individual identities.

Clarity in communication

Complex salary data and workplace metrics are presented in clear, accessible formats. No jargon, no hidden agendas—just straightforward information you can trust.

Inclusive perspective

We amplify diverse voices across industries, roles, and backgrounds. Every employee's experience matters, creating a comprehensive view of workplace culture.
